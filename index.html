About FruityHub

Our Story

FruityHub was founded in 2012 in Kampala, Uganda, by Mr. Samuel Mugisha and Mrs. Grace Nakato. Growing up in Bushenyi District in Western Uganda, both founders witnessed firsthand how local farmers struggled to get fair prices for their high-quality tropical fruits.

Starting with a small collection centre in Nakasero Market, FruityHub has grown into one of Uganda's leading fruit aggregation, processing, and distribution companies. We now operate from our main warehouse in Namanve Industrial Park and maintain collection centres in Mbale, Masaka, Luwero, Fort Portal, and Soroti.

Today, we supply fresh fruits to supermarkets including Shoprite, Carrefour Uganda, and Capital Shoppers, as well as hotels, restaurants, schools, and individual households across the Greater Kampala Metropolitan Area.

Our Mission

To connect Ugandan smallholder fruit farmers to reliable markets while delivering the freshest, highest-quality tropical fruits to consumers at fair prices — promoting healthy eating and sustainable agriculture across East Africa.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted fruit brand by 2030, expanding our operations to Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and South Sudan while maintaining our commitment to organic farming and fair trade practices.
